Nitrgen Dioxide

Nitrogen Dioxid is a special chemical. NO2 (Nitrogen Dioxide) is comprised of one atom of nitrogen and two atoms of oxygen, and is a gas at ambient temperatures. It is an intermediate in the synthesis of nitric acid, millions of tons of which are produced each year, primarily for the production of fertilizers.
